About us

Back in 2012, I was doing research for a presentation and stumbled upon a big problem.

India had tons of people with degrees, but companies couldn't find enough qualified workers! Graduates were stuck without jobs, while big companies didn't hire them.

This surprised me, especially considering how many people live in India.

After digging deeper, I discovered a huge gap in skills: only 3 out of 100 people were considered "ready" for good jobs. Many ended up stuck in low-paying roles like customer service, while countries like the UK and USA zoomed ahead.

I had experience both in business (startups) and teaching (tutoring), and I wanted to use both to help young people succeed.

So, after 7 years in the corporate world, I took a leap - I quit my job and started a learning center called Adventure Academy.

Adventure Academy isn't just about getting good grades.

We help students develop important skills like problem-solving and thinking critically.We encourage them to try new things, learn from their mistakes, and discover what they're good at.

We also have a digital marketing company.

This gives us real-world examples to teach our students, helping them overcome their fears and step into the world of possibilities.

In short, Adventure Academy is all about empowering students to become confident, skilled individuals who can tackle any challenge!

Our vision

We see a talent gap in India. People are bursting with ideas and starting ventures, but finding skilled teams is a struggle. On the other hand, freshers and career seekers are eager for opportunities. They want to jump in and contribute, but lack the skills or the right platform to connect with the right companies.

Here's where we come in. We're building a bridge to close this gap. We equip passionate individuals with the skills they need and connect them with the companies that need them.

Imagine: Confident graduates landing their dream jobs at exciting startups. Imagine innovative companies finding the perfect talent to fuel their growth. That's what we see in the future, and that's what we're working towards every day.

We're not just bridging gaps, we're sparking connections and inspiring success stories.
